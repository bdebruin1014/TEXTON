ADD CUSTOM ACCOUNTS (Optional):
-------------------------------
You can add more accounts following the numbering system.

Example: Want to add another cash account?
1. Find row: 1100-03, Cash - Escrow Account
2. Insert new row after it
3. Add: 1100-04, Cash - Special Account - PELH, 1100, Bank, 0, Asset, No, Your description

Rules:
- Parent account must exist (1100 in this example)
- Use next sequential number (1100-04)
- Keep same structure as other rows

REMOVE UNNECESSARY ACCOUNTS (Optional):
---------------------------------------
You can delete rows for accounts you won't use.

âš ï¸ WARNING: DO NOT delete accounts marked "Yes" in Required column!

Example: Don't need model home accounting?
- Delete rows containing "Model Home"

Safe to remove:
- Accounts with Required = "No"
- Detail accounts (Is Group = 0)
- Optional categories

NOT safe to remove:
- Accounts with Required = "Yes"
- Main category accounts (1000, 2000, 3000, etc.)
- Group accounts with children

---------------------------

â“ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------

Q: Can I use the same template for multiple projects?
A: YES! That's the whole point. Use TEMPLATE_SPE_Lot_Development.csv
   for every lot development project. Just customize the {{ABBR}} and
   member names differently for each.

Q: What if I have 3 partners instead of 2?
A: Add additional member accounts (see "Adding 3rd, 4th, 5th Members" above)

Q: Can I add accounts later after importing?
A: YES! You can add accounts in the system or re-import an updated CSV

Q: Should I use entity suffix ({{ABBR}}) or not?
A: RECOMMENDED: Use it. Makes consolidated reporting much easier.
   You can clearly see which entity each account belongs to.

Q: What if my builder isn't RCH?
A: Do find/replace: {{BUILDER_NAME}} â†’ Your builder abbreviation

Q: Can I remove accounts I don't need?
A: YES, but ONLY accounts marked Required = "No"
   DO NOT remove accounts marked Required = "Yes"

Q: Do I need all 8 columns in the CSV?
A: No - the last 2 columns (Required, Description) are for reference.
   Most systems only need the first 6 columns.

Q: Can I change account numbers?
A: NOT RECOMMENDED. The numbering is industry standard.
   You can rename accounts, but keep numbers consistent.

Q: What if import fails?
A: Check error message, refer to "Troubleshooting" section above
   Most issues: parent accounts missing, duplicates, or formatting

Q: How do I know which template to use?
A: Refer to "STEP 1: SELECT THE RIGHT TEMPLATE" decision tree above
