## DM Budget Standard Cost Structure

For all DM Budget plans (standard scattered lot builds):

(1) Sticks & Bricks: varies by plan (see table above)
(2) Site Specific: $10,875 (survey $1,700, grading $1,700, silt fence $2,000, temp drive $500, landscaping $2,500, flatwork $2,475)
(3) Soft Costs: $2,650 (building permits $2,050, engineering $600) — NOTE: does not include water meter, which varies by county
(4) TOTAL (DM Budget) = S&B + Site Specific + Soft Costs

## RC Pricing Guide Contract Cost Structure

For all RC Pricing Guide contract plans:

(1) Sticks & Bricks: varies by plan
(2) Upgrades: $3,500 base (Hardie Color-Plus minimum)
(3) Lot Prep: $15,000 minimum (contract floor)
(4) Vertical Costs: $0 standard
(5) Soft Costs: $15,000 minimum (contract floor)
(6) Builder Fee: $15,000 fixed
(7) TOTAL = sum of 1 through 6

## Cost Per Square Foot Ranges (DM Budget S&B)

SFH: $64–$96/SF (MAGNOLIA at $64/SF low end, BANYAN at $96/SF high end due to 3-story complexity)
TH: $74–$88/SF typical range
Average SFH: ~$75/SF for 2000+ SF plans, ~$85/SF for 1200-1600 SF plans
Average TH: ~$82/SF

## Historical Cost Trends

From the Trends sheet, costs have been tracked from March 2024 through September 2025. DM Budget pricing reflects September 2025 vendor quotes. Material costs (lumber, roofing) have shown moderate inflation over this period. Always note the cost vintage date in underwriting memos.
