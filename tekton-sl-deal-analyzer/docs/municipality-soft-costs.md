# Municipality Soft Cost Fee Schedules

All fees below are for standard residential new construction (single-family home, 3/4" or 5/8" water meter). Fees are estimates based on published schedules as of 2025. ALWAYS flag in memo that municipality fees must be verified before closing. Fee schedules change frequently. Call before relying on any figure.

## NOTES

(a) SC has no state-level impact fees for residential construction. Fees are set locally.
(b) NC allows System Development Fees under GS 162A Article 8. These can be substantial (see Charlotte Water).
(c) Greenville Water fees are uniform across all Greenville County municipalities served by GW.
(d) ReWa's New Account Fee ($2,500/EDU) replaced the old Capital Contribution Area system as of 7/1/2024.
(e) Spartanburg Water tap fees vary significantly based on distance from main line; always get a site-specific quote.
(f) Charlotte Water FY26 rates are effective 7/1/2025 through 6/30/2026.
(g) Hendersonville SDFs are new as of 1/1/2025; amounts may still be adjusting.
